Welcome to the retreat hackathon!


What you have in your hand is a ceramic pendant that can serve as your crypto identity. You can see it as a badge that represents you are a member of the 3Box Labs team.

The goal of this hackathon is to build something cool which uses your and your team mates pendants using Ceramic & ComposeDB. Bonus points if you integrate with OpenAI or similar AI tools.

The main prize of this hackathon is that your app will be used as the redirect when the chip is scanned. However, there is going to be other prices as well as determined by Kristal.

This hackathon will be ongoing not only during the retreat, but until the beginning of our next season. During the retreat your goal is to form a team, come up with ideas for the hack, and maybe get some code out.
